About Me

Hello there, My name is Lavet Adhiambo, known as code~diva. I 'm born and raised in Nairobi. Who am I? Well I never seem to have a solid answer to that but in a nutshell, I love to think of myself as a tech enthusiast and programming is my voice in this confusing universe. Programming has never been my thing, well at least not until early 2016 around January where I joined Akirachix. Fast forward to now where I’m always trying out new things and trying to stay away from the comfort zone. It’s been a walk across a lego path but an interesting journey. Constantly seeking new adventure , My work focuses on creating awseome web and mobile applications. I engage in product design as well.
